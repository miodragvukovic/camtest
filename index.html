Myofascial Release

The word “myofascial” comes from the Latin words “myo” meaning muscle and “fascia” meaning fibrous bands. Fascia is a connective tissue that surrounds all muscles, tendons, ligaments, bones, and organs within the body. Fascia itself is very strong, flexible, and dynamic, and it responds well to stretching. We’ll use a variety of techniques, including deep and subtle touch, stretching, and movement (“unwinding”) to release chronic holding patterns in the muscular and fascia systems to eliminate pain and facilitate opening. It’s also helpful in treating back and neck pain, headaches, recurring sports injuries, and scoliosis, among other conditions.

Mobility Session

Combine core strength and balance to completely stretch and change your body. In a mobility session our favorite tools are foam rollers, yoga tune up balls, therabands and other massage implements. We mix yoga contact work, assisted stretching, and Yoga Tune Up® ball work to better body alignment. You will emerge feeling balanced, elongated, and relaxed. Please wear yoga or loose-fitting, comfortable clothing for this session.

What is mobility?
You gotta move! Stretching and mobility are the parts of a fitness regimen most often skipped and misunderstood. Simple warm up drills help mobility but they don’t go far enough. Stretching only focuses on lengthening short and tight muscles. Mobility work, on the other hand, is a movement-based integrated full-body approach that addresses all the elements that limit movement and performance.

Booking Appointments

Booking an appointment online? No problem! After we’ve confirmed your appointment on our side, we’ll respond in email with your confirmation number. If you don’t receive one, please try again. Otherwise, please call, send a text message, or email Bridget. She’ll take care of you. Gift certificates are available upon request.

Payment and Timing

Our goal at Body Lab is to make our services affordable, and we do so by keeping overhead costs low. So, we prefer payment with either cash or a check (although if you forget your checkbook, we also offer credit transactions). Please plan on arriving 10 minutes before your scheduled appointment. We have a sweet waiting area, where you can unplug, decompress, and quiet the mind.

Cancellations

Time is valuable, both yours and ours. To that end, we reserve your time slot for you, and that reserved time is unavailable to another client. However, we also understand the need to cancel, and we maintain a 24-hour cancellation policy. If you cancel less than 24 hours in advance, we charge 50% of the total cost of the appointment. No-shows must pay in full.
